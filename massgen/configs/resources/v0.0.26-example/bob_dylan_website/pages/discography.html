Selected Discography

The Freewheelin' Bob Dylan (1963)

A seminal album featuring iconic protest songs like "Blowin' in the Wind" and "A Hard Rain's A-Gonna Fall."

Highway 61 Revisited (1965)

Marked his bold transition to electric rock, with tracks like "Like a Rolling Stone" and "Desolation Row."

Blonde on Blonde (1966)

Often considered one of the first double albums in rock history, a masterpiece of lyrical complexity and musical diversity.

Blood on the Tracks (1975)

A deeply personal and critically acclaimed work, often cited as one of his greatest, dealing with themes of heartbreak and relationships.

Time Out of Mind (1997)

A late-career resurgence, winning multiple Grammy Awards and showcasing a darker, more reflective side.
